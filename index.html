Introduction

In reality, there is no real me. I act and speak differently.
The majority of the population will probably say,
"Doesn't everyone do that in this generation?"
The answer might be "yes,"
but my case is just a little different.

I have such a tendency to lose interest in people quickly and cut them off.
I know it's not me; it's something else that's harder to control,
but sometimes it's perfectly all right to do so.
...

The main challenge is that, although being an introvert,
I act openly and have a habit of cutting people off.

The last concert I visited,
I did nothing except stand there and participate in conversations;
nevertheless, I cut them off once they became my friends.

Overall, cutting them off is neither illegal nor embarrassing.
